Simple Scheduler Calendar App: User Guide

Welcome to the Simple Scheduler Calendar App! This guide will walk you through the app's features and how to use them.

1. Navigating the Calendar

  • Viewing the Calendar: The app displays the current year's calendar. The current date is highlighted. Past dates are disabled (grayed out).
  • Moving Between Months: Use the left (<) and right (>) arrow buttons to navigate. You can only navigate to future months within the current year.

2. Booking an Appointment

  • Finding Available Slots: Available slots are marked with a "+" icon on Mondays, Tuesdays, and Wednesdays. Only one booking per day is allowed.
  • Selecting a Date: Click a "+" to open the booking form.
  • Filling the Form:
    • Name: Enter your full name.
    • Phone Number: Enter your phone number (used for booking identification).
    • Age: Enter your age.
    • Gender: Select your gender.
  • Adding Extra People (Optional):
    • Click "Add Extra Person" to add details for up to two people.
    • For each person, enter their age and select their gender.
  • Submitting: Click "Submit" to confirm.
  • Confirmation: A "Booking confirmed!" message appears briefly. The "+" icon disappears.
  • Canceling: Contact the administrator to cancel. No-shows are considered cancellations.

Only applicable for admin User

3. Viewing Current Month's Bookings

  • Accessing Bookings: Open the bookings.html page.
  • Viewing Bookings: The table shows bookings for the current month (name, phone, age, gender, date, extra people details).

4. Exporting and Importing Bookings (Backup/Restore)

  • Exporting Bookings:
    1. On bookings.html, click "Export Bookings".
    2. Save the downloaded bookings.json file to your computer.
  • Importing Bookings:
    1. On bookings.html, click "Import Bookings".
    2. Select the bookings.json file you previously saved.
    3. Your bookings will be restored.

5. Important Notes

  • Bookings are stored locally in your browser. Export regularly for backup.
  • Only one booking per phone number per day is allowed. Book again after your previous booking date.
  • Contact the administrator for questions or assistance.
